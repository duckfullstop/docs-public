# What I promise
Simple, down-to-earth help with your tech needs, with no pressure selling or unnecessary overspend.
# What I do
* I'm happy to consult with you for **support with any PC, Mac, or Linux queries** you might have, be they hardware or software (or both!)
	* This include any hardware or software upgrades you may desire.
* I can also provide **support with iPhone and Android software**, as well as more arcane older stuff (just try and stump me!)
* I can work with you on **custom PC builds**, for anything from standard day-to-day desktop use to high-powered gaming and workstations.
* I can advise, support, and install **smart home equipment**, including full solutions based on _Home Assistant_.
	* This includes custom, fully-addressable LED strip installations of any length or complexity.
* I can advise on, install, and support **Ubiquiti networking equipment**, as well as home / small business networks.
	* This includes custom ethernet runs, whole-home WiFi, and more.
* I can do **urgent call-outs** when possible, but please note that due to personal health and life reasons I cannot guarantee availability of this service.
* I can provide **custom scripts or small applications** for niche needs - **price on application**.
# What I don't do
* Business deployments at scale (including domains / active directory) - you should probably be using a proper IT solutions provider.
* Work requiring a qualified electrician (e.g on the mains side)
* Challenging hardware installations (for example, those at height)

## My background
I've been doing tech stuff for well over half my life at this point (20 years plus!), with a background in software engineering (in Python, PHP, and Go, if you're curious), [LAN parties](https://thebiggame.org), arcade maintenance and operations, and much more. I routinely share my skills at my local Repair Café for those that come to us in need.

My rates reflect the level of experience and confidence I have in each area of my skillset.
