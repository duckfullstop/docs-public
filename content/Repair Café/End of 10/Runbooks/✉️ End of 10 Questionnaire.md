## Intention

This Questionnaire is intended for any customer who enquires regarding _End of 10_ support as an initial triage tool.

## Questionnaire

**Question 1**: Is your system running Windows 10 at present?
* Yes, it is
* I'm not sure what version I'm running

**Question 2**: Do you know whether it can be upgraded to Windows 11?
* Yes, it can
* No, it definitely cannot, because (reason)
* Unsure / I don't know how to find out!

**Question 3**: Do you know how to enable Extended Support for your Windows 10 System?
* Yes, and I've turned it on
* Yes, but I haven't turned it on yet because (reason)
* No, I don't know how to do this

**Question 4**: Do you know your system's specifications? Please provide a system model number or component parts (if known).

**Question 5**: On a scale of 1-5 (with 1 being very little and 5 being very competent), how technically minded do you consider yourself?
* 1: I barely understand the basics!
* 2: I know a little bit about PCs (for example, how to do updates & basic maintenance)
* 3: I'm reasonably confident with my PC (for example, I know how to manage software)
* 4: I'm fairly skilled (for example, I know how to troubleshoot errors)
* 5: I'm proficient (for example, I know how to use the terminal / can write software)

**Question 6**: On a scale of 1-5 (with 1 being not open and 5 being very open), how open are you to learning new ways of working with your system?
* 1: I much prefer things being exactly the way as they are now.
* 2
* 3
* 4
* 5: I love and embrace change!

**Question 7**: If it was determined that you would require a replacement system (either new or used), would you consider it economically challenging to purchase one?
* I would be able to finance a new system
* I would be able to finance a used system
* I would prefer not to have to purchase a new system if it can be at all avoided
* I'd prefer not to answer this question

**Question 8**: Would you be able to bring your system to the next café for further discussion & diagnostics if necessary?
* Yes - it's a laptop or other portable system
* Yes - it's a desktop, but I can transport it (we will provide a monitor, keyboard, and mouse)
* No

**Question 9**: Do you have a full backup of your system?

* Yes, but I don't presently have one
* No, but I know how to make one
* No, and I have no idea where to start!
## Implementation Notes

Questions 1 and 2: This is an initial screener just to ensure this is actually an _End of 10_ issue / get some basic understanding of the user's skill level.

Question 3: If ES is already enabled, then this is a less urgent issue for the Café.

Question 4: This may give us an early indication as to whether the system can be hardware upgraded / whether it's a Secure Boot issue or similar.

Questions 5 and 6: These screen for an idea of what complexity we can talk to the user with / whether they are a potential candidate for learning a new OS if necessary.

Question 7: This screens for a direction of travel if we are unable to upgrade the system. If there is financial capability to upgrade the system, we can give them resources to guide their purchasing decisions. If there is not, we can more strongly consider the Alternative OS options (depending on Question 5) or forced upgrades. See the **📔 Repairer's Guide**.

Question 8: This just gives us an idea whether this will be a task for the next café - and whether someone needs to bring a display, keyboard, and mouse.

Question 9: Negative responses to this mean we'll send some basic information / links to guidance on performing backups.
