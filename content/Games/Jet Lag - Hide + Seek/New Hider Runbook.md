The hider gets a maximum of **10 minutes** preparation time after the previous hider is found. They can choose to start early if it's advantageous to them; the clock starts when they leave or after 10 minutes, whichever comes first.

**Remember to:**
* [ ] Have the new Hider turn off Find My - Seekers to ensure sharing with Hider, other Seeker, and third party, Hider to ensure sharing with third party only
* [ ] Shuffle the deck
* [ ] Hand shuffled deck to new hider
* [ ] Ensure hider & seekers have at least three dice per side
* [ ] Start stopwatch (all participants) and note departure time in the group chat
