# Recipes

Recipes are organised into categories depending on overall difficulty:
* Keep it Simple #recipe/basic
	* *These are straightforward recipes, suitable for beginners.*
	* *If you're super new, try recipes marked "🔰 Perfect for beginners"* and with the tag #recipe/basic/beginners - these include extra pointers and hand-holding.
* I know what I'm doing #recipe/intermediate
	* *These recipes require a little bit of skill and confidence, but are generally pretty quick and straightforward otherwise.*
* I wanna work for it! #recipe/advanced
	* *These recipes are more complex, and may require more advanced skills or utilities, or take a substantial amount of time to complete. The payoff's worth it, though!*
