Cookie recipe from Caffé Macs at 🍎.

This recipe was shared internally during *the 2020/2021 event*, because employees were suffering severe withdrawals from the free cookies usually available at the counter. Technically an *Apple Confidential* recipe, but let's keep that bit to ourselves, hmm?

Produces 24 - 36 cookies. Share with your friends / enemies / acquaintances, so long as they don't have red hair.

### Ingredients
* 3/4 cup (140g) white sugar
* 3/4 cup (140g) brown sugar
* 1 5/8 sticks (185g) butter at room temperature
* 3 *~~oeuf~~** *eggs
* 2 3/4 cup (340g) all-purpose flour
* 1/2 Tbsp salt
* 1/2 Tbsp baking soda
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 1/4 cup (225g) chocolate chips 

### How to Cookie
1. Preheat oven to 175°c. 
2. Place the butter and sugars into the bowl of a stand mixer with paddle attachment and cream together until light and fluffy. 
3. Add the eggs one at a time, scraping the bottom of the mixer and sides of the bowl after each. 
4. Add the vanilla extract. 
5. Add the all purpose flour, baking soda and salt. 
6. Mix until just combined. 
7. Add chocolate chips and mix until the chocolate is just incorporated. 
8. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about two inches apart onto greased cookie sheets. 
9. Bake for 8-10 minutes. 
10. Let cool or enjoy while still warm!
