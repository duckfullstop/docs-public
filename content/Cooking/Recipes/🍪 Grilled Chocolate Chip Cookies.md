Makes around 12-15 cookies.

### Ingredients

* 220g Salted Butter
* Two Cups Sugar
* ~ Tablespoon Dark Treacle (🇺🇸 Molasses) (or less, **don’t** measure it[^1])
* Two Eggs
* Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
* Two Teaspoons Salt
* Teaspoon Bicarbonate of Soda (🇺🇸 Baking Soda)
* Two to three cups bread flour
* ~ 340g Chocolate Chips

Melt butter completely, then mix in sugar and treacle. Mix until wet brown sugar is achieved, then add eggs and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth, then add salt, baking powder, and two cups of flour. Mix rigorously, and add more flour slowly (usually around half a cup) until the dough looks wet, but isn’t sticky (pulls away from the fingers). Mix in chocolate chips, then refrigerate dough for half an hour.

Preheat grill on top shelf

Roll into golf balls and squeeze down into hockey pucks, then place on a parchment lined baking tray and grill at high intensity for around 1-2 minutes until top goes golden. Remove, set oven to 190c baking, wait for grill to cool down, then bake on bottom or middle shelf for 10-15 minutes. Don’t overbake - pull early and evaluate if unsure.

[^1]: Treacle is extraordinarily sticky - if you try to measure it you're just going to be left standing there with half of it stuck to the spoon, waiting an eternity for it to drip off, and with another sticky mess to clean up afterwards.
