## What I can do
* (Up to) C0 inkjet plotting or printing
* 300dpi, 600dpi, 600dpi High-Quality, or 1400dpi Line Drawing
* Banner printing (infinite length in the Y axis up to 20 metres)
* Pricing the same regardless of colour or black & white
* Shipping either in-person or via Royal Mail Tube service - price on application
* Same-day turnaround / dispatch if possible & needed - no guarantees, please give me as much notice as possible!
## What I can't do
* Ultra-high-resolution photos, for gallery display etc
	* CMYK can put some pretty good quality out, but 12-colour professional photo printers are much better suited for that kind of task.
	* If you just want something to casually hang on your wall, my service should be fine.
* I can't guarantee colour accuracy
* Prints exceeding 20 metres in length, except in exceptional circumstances
## What gear I use
Canon iPF770 - A0, 5-ink continuous feed printer (CMY, PigK, K)
